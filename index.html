Fabian Mentzer

I received my PhD at the Computer Vision Lab of ETH Zurich, were I was supervised by Prof. Luc Van Gool. The focus of my PhD was neural compression, and I explored learned lossy and lossless image compression. Prior to working towards a PhD, I recieved a Bachelor and Master of Science from ETH Zurich, in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology. This is my personal homepage.
